What's lurking in the back of your fridge?

What's For Lunch turns your refrigerator from a mystery box into an organized inventory. Scan QR stickers, photograph food, scan barcodes — the app tracks everything and tells you what to eat before it goes bad.

**Track in seconds**
• Stick a QR code on any container and scan it open
• Photograph leftovers — AI identifies what's inside and estimates how long it's safe
• Scan product barcodes to add packaged items instantly
• Type it in manually when you want full control

**Never waste food again**
• Color-coded freshness: green → yellow → red as food approaches expiry
• Daily reminders for items about to go bad
• Mark items eaten, tossed, frozen, or partially used
• See your waste history and track your progress over time

**Share with your household**
• Real-time sync so everyone sees the same fridge
• Household members can add, update, and consume items
• Know what you need before going grocery shopping

**Powered by AI**
• AI recognizes food from photos and estimates safe-to-eat windows
• Learns from common household foods — no manual lookup needed
• You're always in control — accept or override any suggestion

**Offline-first**
• Add items without internet — syncs when you reconnect
• Full inventory available even without a connection

---

Privacy-first: your fridge contents stay private. We don't sell your data. We don't train on your photos. Full privacy policy at app.whatsforlunch.app/privacy.
