## Getting Started

**Q: What does WhatsForLunch do?**

WhatsForLunch tracks the food in your fridge, freezer, and pantry. It tells you what's expiring soon so you can use it up before it goes bad — and it helps you plan meals around what you already have.

**Q: Is WhatsForLunch free?**

The core features are free — you can add unlimited food items, get expiry alerts, and share a household with your family. The Premium plan ($4.99/month or $39.99/year) adds AI-powered food classification from photos, recipe suggestions, and grocery receipt scanning.

**Q: How do I sign up?**

Enter your email on the sign-in screen and tap "Send magic link." We'll email you a one-tap link — no password to remember.

**Q: Can I use Apple Sign-In or Google?**

Yes. Tap "Continue with Apple" or "Continue with Google" on the sign-in screen.

## Adding Food

**Q: How do I add a food item?**

Tap the "+" button on the home screen. You can:
- Type the food name manually
- Scan a barcode (Premium)
- Take a photo for AI classification (Premium)

**Q: How does AI classification work?**

Point your camera at the food item. Our AI identifies what it is (e.g., "Leftover Pasta") and looks up the safe storage time from our food safety database. It's not perfect — always use your judgment.

**Q: What if the AI gets it wrong?**

Tap the food name to edit it. You can also change the food type, storage location, and expiry date manually.

**Q: Can I add items without an expiry date?**

Yes. Pantry items like onions, garlic, and nuts don't need an expiry date — leave it blank or set one manually.

**Q: What is "quantity text"?**

A free-text note about how much you have (e.g., "half a block," "3 servings," "opened"). We don't do exact measurements — it's a reminder, not a recipe planner.

## Expiry & Notifications

**Q: How does WhatsForLunch know when food expires?**

We use a database of ~150 food types with safe storage times from USDA and food safety guidelines. For items you classify with AI, the AI estimates the time. You can always override the date manually.

**Q: When do I get notified?**

You'll receive a push notification when an item expires within 24 hours. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

**Q: I got a notification but the food looks fine — is it safe?**

Our dates are conservative estimates, not hard rules. Use your senses — if it smells and looks fine, it probably is. The notification is a prompt to check, not a command to toss.

**Q: Why aren't I getting notifications?**

Check:
1. Settings → Notifications → WhatsForLunch — should be "Allow"
2. Do Not Focus mode is off (or has an exception for WFL)
3. App version is up to date

If still not working, tap the notification icon in the app to manually review expiring items.

## Households & Sharing

**Q: What is a "household"?**

A household is a shared food inventory. Everyone in the household sees the same items. Use it for roommates, family members, or your own home setup.

**Q: How do I invite someone to my household?**

Go to Household Settings → Members → Invite. Share the link via text, email, or AirDrop. The person must have the app installed.

**Q: Can I be in more than one household?**

Yes. You can be a member of multiple households — useful if you have a work fridge and a home fridge, or if you share with both roommates and a partner.

**Q: What are the different member roles?**

- **Owner** — can rename the household, invite/remove members, and delete the household
- **Member** — can add, edit, and delete items
- **Viewer** — can see items but not edit them (useful for a landlord or caretaker)

## Account & Privacy

**Q: What data do you store?**

We store your email, food items, and household membership. We don't sell your data. See our [Privacy Policy](https://whatsforlunch.app/privacy) for details.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data is soft-deleted immediately (you can't sign in), then permanently purged after 30 days per our data retention policy. This complies with GDPR and CCPA.

**Q: Can I export my data before deleting?**

Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Export My Data. We'll generate a JSON file with all your food items and household history and email you a download link.

**Q: I forgot my email or can't receive the magic link — what do I do?**

Check your spam folder first. If the email still doesn't arrive within 2 minutes, try a different email address. If you're locked out, email support@whatsforlunch.app and we'll manually verify your identity.

## Subscription & Billing

**Q: How much does Premium cost?**

- Monthly: $4.99/month
- Annual: $39.99/year (save 33%)

**Q: What's included in Premium?**

- AI photo classification (scan a plate or container)
- Barcode scanning for packaged goods
- Recipe suggestions based on what's about to expire
- Unlimited AI classifications per day (free plan: 5/day)

**Q: Can I try Premium for free?**

Yes. New users get a 7-day free trial of Premium. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged.

**Q: How do I cancel?**

Premium is managed by Apple or Google depending on how you subscribed:
- iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → WhatsForLunch → Cancel
- Android: Play Store → Subscriptions → WhatsForLunch → Cancel

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**

Refunds are handled by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). We can't issue refunds directly. Contact Apple support or Google Play support for refund requests.

## Technical

- iPhone running iOS 16 or later
- Android phones running Android 10 or later

Yes. You can add and edit items without an internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**

- Include your device model, iOS/Android version, and app version (Settings → About)

**Q: How often is the app updated?**

We ship updates roughly every 2 weeks. Critical bug fixes ship as hotfixes within 48 hours.
