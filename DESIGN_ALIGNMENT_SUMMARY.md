## 🎨 Design Tokens Applied

### Colors Used

- **Brand:** `#0E5C3A` (primary), `#08402A` (dark), `#1F8B5C` (light), `#2DBC83` (glow)
- **Accents:** Coral (#FF6B47), Honey (#F4B942), Berry (#C2185B), Sky (#4A90E2), Plum (#6B5B95)
- **Status:** Fresh (#1F9956), Soon (#E08F1B), Urgent (#E0392B), Expired (#6B6B6B)
- **Surface:** Base (#FAF6EE), Raised (#FFFFFF), Sunken (#F5F1E5)
- **Text:** Primary (#0F1A11), Secondary (#4D5A4F), Tertiary (#7B8580)

### Typography Applied

- **Display/Titles:** Fraunces serif, 800 weight, negative letter-spacing
  - H1: 34px, -1.2px
  - H2: 28px, -0.8px
  - H3: 22px, -0.4px
- **Body:** Inter sans-serif, 400-700 weight
- **Caption:** 12px, 600 weight, 0.3px tracking

### Spacing Applied

- Radii: 8px (xs), 12px (sm), 16px (md), 22px (lg), 32px (xl), 9999px (full)
- Padding: Consistent 22px horizontal, 16-24px vertical
- Gaps: 8-14px between elements

### Shadows Applied

- e1: Subtle (1-2px drops)
- e2: Medium (8px drop)
- e3: Large (20px drop)
- glow: Brand glow (8px 32px, 25% opacity)
- coral: Coral glow (8px 24px, 30% opacity)
